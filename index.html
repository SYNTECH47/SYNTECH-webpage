Who are we?

We are Syntech. A technology club from University Technology Petronas. We aim to provide our members with the necessary knowledge to use the latest and greatest software development tools. We also pursue tangent projects like community service projects to provide our members with a holistic skill development opportunity.

Recent events

Syntech in collaboration with the STEM for the Future community service team, held a technology fair in SMK Malim Nawar. The school students had the opportunity to fidget with the various robots and smart devices provided by Syntech and the sommunity service team.

The Syntech Flutter workshop is a workshop opened to all UTP students that aims to teach cross platform app development using the Flutter framework. Over 40 participants joined the 4 hour workshop.

Over 50 highshool student participated in the Syntech Arduino workshop aimed at teaching and showcasing the Arduino microcontroller for various IOT applications.
